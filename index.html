The whole method · about 90 seconds

Slow · Say · Sense · Scan

You can't think your way calm. Your body listens to your breath, your weight, and your attention — the method is simply how you use all three.

Slow

Two short breaths in — then a long breath out. The fastest way down.

Say

Name the state in three words. “Chest tight, wired.” The state, not the story.

Sense

Feel one point of contact. Feet on the floor. Press in. Let it hold you.

Scan

Look slowly around. Name three things you can see. You're here, and here is okay.
